# Verity: AI-Powered Truth for Your Professional Feed

**Cut through the noise. Fact-check LinkedIn posts instantly with AI.**

Verity is a powerful Chrome extension designed to bring clarity and accountability to your professional networking. In an era of viral misinformation and sophisticated rhetorical "hacks," Verity helps you identify what's real, what's misleading, and what's just plain wrong—all without leaving your LinkedIn feed.

---

## 🛡️ What Verity Does for You

Verity acts as your personal intelligence analyst while you browse LinkedIn. With a single click, it dissects posts to provide:

*   **Truth Verification**: We extract specific claims and cross-reference them with credible, high-authority sources (government reports, academic papers, and primary news).
*   **Logic Analysis**: Not all arguments are built equal. Verity identifies logical fallacies—like "straw man" arguments or "ad hominem" attacks—that writers use to manipulate your opinion.
*   **Trust Score**: Get an instant 0-100 logic score to see how much weight you should really give to a post's reasoning.
*   **Transparent Sourcing**: We don't just tell you it's true or false; we provide direct links to the evidence so you can see for yourself.

## 🚀 How to Install (Quick Start)

Since Verity is currently in its early release, you can install it manually in just a few seconds:

1.  **Download the Release**: Download the latest `verity-extension.zip` from our [Releases page](https://github.com/k-zeybek/verity/releases) (or download the code and use the `extension` folder).
2.  **Unzip the File**: Extract the contents to a folder on your computer.
3.  **Open Extensions Page**: In Google Chrome, go to `chrome://extensions/`.
4.  **Enable Developer Mode**: Flip the toggle in the **top-right corner** to the "On" position.
5.  **Load the Extension**: Click the **"Load unpacked"** button and select the folder where you unzipped the extension.
6.  **Start Fact-Checking**: Navigate to [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com) and look for the shield icon (🛡️) on any post!

---

## 📝 Configuration
You can customize Verity by clicking the extension icon in your toolbar:
- **Theme Selection**: Switch between Light, Dark, or System mode.
- **Language Preferences**: Force the AI to respond in your preferred language.

---

## 🛡️ Privacy & Safety
Verity only reads the text of the post you explicitly click to analyze. We don't track your browsing history or collect personal data. Your professional privacy is our priority.
